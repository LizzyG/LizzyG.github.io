OFFLINE

Development, LLC

At Offline Development (or O.L.D. as we sometimes call it) our driving mission is to harness the power of the internet to improve our offline(real) lives.  We’re not trying to “drive engagement” or “increase sales.”  We’re trying to get some value out of the vast information available on the internet, then get off the internet and go have fun.  They may be going meta, but we’re going IRL.

We store as little information about you as possible to make our software function, we don’t track you and won’t sell your information.  Our model user is ourselves, we make software we want to use.

Our first product is NearHear.  Ever wanted to see live music but didn’t know where to go?  NearHear scours the internet for show listings, uses streaming platforms to let you hear all your options, then sends you out into the world to see real live music.  Try it, you’ll like it.  

O.L.D. is Liz (founder, engineer, and boss bitch) and Dan (designer, engineer and gullible optimist).
